Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new librarian?

To add a new librarian, click on the "Add" button in the toolbar and fill out the required information in the form that appears.

How do I delete a librarian?

To delete a librarian, click on the "Delete" button next to the librarian you wish to remove. Confirm the action in the popup that appears.

How do I search for a user?

Use the search bar at the top of the page to find users by entering their email or ID. The table will automatically filter the results based on your input.

How do I check the availability of a book?

To check the availability of a book, enter the book's title or ISBN in the search bar. The system will display the current status of the book.

How do I reserve a book?

To reserve a book, search for the book and click on the "Reserve" button. You will receive a notification once the book is available for pickup.

How do I renew a borrowed book?

To renew a borrowed book, go to your account section and click on "Renew" next to the book you wish to extend. Note that renewals are subject to availability.

How do I update my user profile?

To update your user profile, log in to your account and go to the profile section. Here, you can update your personal information and preferences.

How do I reset my password?

If you have forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

Dashboard

The dashboard provides a summary of the library's key metrics and quick access to important functions.

Admin Menu

The admin menu allows you to manage librarians, users, and library settings. Access this menu from the top-right corner of the screen.

Cataloging

In the cataloging section, you can add, update, or delete book records. Ensure all necessary information is filled out before saving a book entry.

Checkout

The checkout process allows you to lend books to users. Scan the book's barcode and the user's library card to complete a checkout.

Login

To access the library management system, log in with your credentials. Ensure your password is secure and updated regularly.

Manage Requests

Manage book requests from users in this section. Approve or deny requests and notify users about the status of their requests.

User Management

In the user management section, you can add, update, or delete user profiles. Ensure user data is accurate and up-to-date.

Welcome Page

The welcome page provides an overview of the library management system and quick access to various sections. Customize this page to suit your needs.
